Top Artists

1. Noisecream

Noisecream is a music composer/sound designer from Russia who creates electronic, cyberpunk, and synthwave music.

Although he has composed his own singles, Noisecream is well known for creating music for indie videogames including:

  • Roboquest
  • Midnight Fight Express
  • My Friend Pedro

2. Mick Gordon

Mick Gordon is a popular Australian composer, sound designer, record producer, and musician who mainly composes metal music for videogames.

He began producing music for videogames with Killer Instinct (2013), but has become famous for composing the soundtrack of DOOM (2016). Other games he has composed for include:

  • Wolfenstein: The New Order
  • Wolfenstein: The New Colossus
  • Prey
  • DOOM Eternal

3. Metallica

Metallica are a famous American heavy metal band that was formed in 1981. Since 1981, they have had a total of eight members, including:

  • James Hetfield
  • Kirk Hammett
  • Lars Ulrich
  • Robert Trujillo
  • Cliff Burton
  • Dave Mustaine
  • Jason Newsted
  • Ron McGovney

Over their long career, Metallica have made several famous albums and songs, with some of their most famous songs including:

  • For Whom the Bell Tolls
  • Seek and Destroy
  • Enter Sandman
  • Whiplash
  • Creeping Death
  • 72 Seasons

4. Processor

Processor is a group of two sound engineers from Sweden who produce industrial metal, electronic, and funk music.

The pair began their career with the release of their debut album Insomnia in 2004. Since then, they have released many popular albums, which include:

  • Red Machine
  • Justice
  • Majesty
  • Entourage

5. A Yarmak

A Yarmak is a music producer from Ukraine that has produced metal and rock music since 2021, with some notable albums being:

  • Roots Grow Deep
  • No Pity for the Sinking Ship

However, A Yarmak also creates metal remixes of songs from popular games, including:

  • Cyberpunk 2077
  • Katana Zero
  • Payday
  • Hotline Miami
